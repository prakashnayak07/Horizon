The framework

Three pillars, one record per job.

Carbon offset

Verified offsets calculated against substrate, run length and freight. Maths attached to every quote — no after-the-fact accounting.

Substrate transparency

FSC-certified stocks by default, with recycled and post-consumer options on every quote. The substrate choice is documented in the job ticket.

SGP-certified supply chain

100% of production routed through Sustainable Green Print certified facilities — audited annually, no exceptions.

The maths

How the offset is calculated.

  1. Step 1 — Substrate footprint

    Paper, ink, plates, energy.

    Tonnes of substrate × emissions factor (mill of origin, pulp source, recycled %).

  2. Step 2 — Production footprint

    Press energy, waste, freight.

    Run length × press kWh, audited waste streams, distance from press to delivery dock.

  3. Step 3 — Offset purchase

    Carbon Neutral, Gold Standard.

    Offsets retired against the calculated tCO₂e — certificate ID attached to the job ticket.

  4. Step 4 — Reforestation

    Native Australian trees, planted.

    For every tonne of paper consumed, one native tree funded through Carbon Neutral's plant-a-tree program.
