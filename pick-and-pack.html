Service 08

Pick & pack.

From single-item dispatches to multi-piece kits and full campaign packs — we pick, assemble, pack and ship out of our Warwick Farm warehouse with tracking on every parcel.

Get a quote How it works

What it is

Kits assembled, parcels out the door.

We hold your stock — print, merch, samples, swag — and turn orders into ready-to-ship parcels. Single items, complex multi-component kits or store-by-store campaign packs, all picked accurately and packed so they arrive properly.

Connect orders via our portal, API, EDI or just send a spreadsheet — whatever's easiest for your team.
