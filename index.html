About HPM

Trusted. Fair.
Proudly independent.

25yrs Established 2001 · Privately held · Australian owned

Horizon Print Management is the strategic print partner for Australian businesses that take their brand seriously. We don't run a press — we run procurement, production oversight, and supply‑chain logistics on your behalf.

That distance from the press is the point. Because we're supplier‑neutral, every job goes to the most appropriate producer at the right price. We work with production printers like Westman Printing, digital specialists like Digital Press, and merchandise partners like Cups Galore — without ever being captive to one of them.
