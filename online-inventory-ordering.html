Online inventory ordering.

Live stock levels for every branded item you hold with us — uniforms, POS kits, brochures, merch. Your team reorders online and finance gets full usage reports.

Book a demo How it works

What it is

Live stock, easy reorders.

We warehouse your printed and branded items, give your team an online catalogue with live stock counts, and ship orders out as they come in. Low-stock alerts trigger automatic reprints so nothing runs out.

Pair it with the print-on-demand portal and you've got both — held stock for things you order often, on-demand print for everything else.
