Our story

We don't run a press —
we run your print programme.

Horizon Print Management is the strategic print partner for Australian businesses that take their brand seriously. We run procurement, production oversight, and supply-chain logistics on your behalf.

That distance from the press is the point. Because we're supplier-neutral, every job goes to the most appropriate producer at the right price — production printers, digital specialists, merchandise partners — without ever being captive to one of them.

What we stand for

Three principles, applied to every job.

Supplier neutrality

No captive presses, no kickbacks. Every job goes to the producer best matched to the brief — measured against your benchmark.

Brand consistency

Documented specs, calibrated colour control, pre-press audits and on-press sign-off — your brand standards enforced identically across every press, every state.

Measured outcomes

Quarterly transparency reports prove the line-item savings, the substrate substitutions, the carbon offset — nothing hidden.

Milestones

  1. 2001

    Horizon founded

    Established in Sydney as Australia's first dedicated supplier-neutral print procurement partner.

    Formal colour-control and pre-press audit framework introduced across the supplier panel.

    Every quote includes verified offsets; production routed through SGP-certified suppliers by default.
